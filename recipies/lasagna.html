Lasagna

Ingredients Required

  • Italian Sausage - 2 lbs
  • Onion - 1
  • Garlic - 1/2 clove
  • Crushed Tomatoes - 1 Can
  • Tomato Paste - 2 cans
  • Tomato Sauce - 2 cans
  • Sugar - 2 tbsps
  • Lasgna Noodles - 1 Box
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta cheese blend - 2 lbs
  • Egg - 1

Steps

  1. Cook Meat Sauce
  2. Cook Noodles
  3. Layer Lasagna noodles and sauce
  4. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes @ 375
  5. Let Lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving
